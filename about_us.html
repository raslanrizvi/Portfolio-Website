Who we are

Crown bicycle bike shop is your destination for the latest bikes, Crown bicycle accessories and professional bike fit services through Crown Precision Fit. Whether you visit a Crown-owned store or one of our awesome independent partners, you’ll find amazing service, knowledgeable staff, and top-quality bikes and gear that you’ll enjoy for years to come. Use our interactive retailer locator to find all the Trek and Electrical bike shops near you.

Vision

We are a passionate group of cyclists and are committed to fostering a cycling community around each of our stores. We believe cycling should be a viable option for everyone and look to keep people riding no matter what happens.

Mission

We stock the worlds best bike brands and accessories with the aim to cater to any type of cyclist no matter the level.
